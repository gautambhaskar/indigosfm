CTE @ FLOWER MOUND HS



ABOUT US

Flower Mound High School is a strong supporter of STEM and CTE programs. The school offers a multitude of classes ranging from marketing to architecture and engineering graphics.

COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer Science is a class offered at FMHS where the teacher, Mrs. Hitch, educates her students in programming and scripting languages such as Java, HTML, CSS, and C++.

ROBOTICS

At the center of the STEM and CTE Programs is the Robotics Club. Mr. Szydlik teaches students how to construct, plan, and design successful robotics. Students from this club have gone to compete in prestigious tournaments like the FRC World Championships, VEX Nationals and Worlds Competitions, as well as the TSA VEX State Competition where our teams have regularly placed in the top 5.

ENGINEERING

The Engineering Design course by our TSA sponsor Mr. Szydlik is a comprehensive class that educates students in professional software such as Autodesk CAD and Inventor. In conjunction, he uses his experience to explain hand drawing to the students.

CAREER CENTERS

The Lewisville Independent School District provides two off campus career centers for further development of career and technology skills such as video game design, biotechnology, architecture, mechanics, and cosmetology. Some of these courses offer certification and licensing in their specific areas. In addition, college credit can be earned for completion of these courses.
