MATHEW

Mathew Abraham is one of the main rappers of the INDIGOS. He is a song writer along with Shaan Jani and enjoys rap and hip hop culture. Mat enjoys writing fast raps with a focus of MC skill and technicality. While his friends know him as Mat, his millions of fans adore him as "MattThaRapper". Many rappers like to brag about their ability to write their raps in studios but Mathew takes it one step further; all his raps were written in the shower.
