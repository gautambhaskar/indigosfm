SHAAN

Shaan Jani, or LilShaan, is a rapper in the INDIGOS. He enjoys writing more melodic raps with an emphasis on substantive, meaningful lyrics. Shaan and Mat's contrasting rap styles adds diversity to the INDIGOS lineup making it one of the most interesting rap groups in history. In addition to being a song writer and rapper with Mat, Shaan plays the guitar and is the lead instrumentalist of the INDIGOS.
